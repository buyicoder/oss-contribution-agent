# Maintainer Reply Workflow

Use this workflow when a maintainer responds to an issue, PR, or review thread.

## Classify the Reply

### Simple Positive

Examples: thanks, looks good, merged, small appreciation.

Action: reply briefly and naturally when a reply adds warmth.

### Small Change Request

Examples: wording tweak, extra test, file move, minor formatting.

Action: make the change if safe, explain briefly, and update the PR.

### Clarification Needed

Examples: maintainer asks why, asks for repro, asks for compatibility details.

Action: answer with evidence and links. Avoid overstating certainty.

### Design or Scope Change

Examples: alternative architecture, larger feature, API compatibility concern.

Action: pause and ask the human.

### L4/L5 Opportunity

Examples: maintainer invites deeper implementation, architecture discussion, ownership of a feature area.

Action: notify the human that the repository may be entering L5 preparation.

## Reply Style

- Keep replies short.
- Reference specific files or behavior when helpful.
- Do not mention automation.
- Do not over-apologize.
- Do not pressure maintainers for review.

## Example Reply

```text
Thanks for taking a look. That makes sense; I adjusted the wording to keep the docs closer to the existing terminology and left the scope limited to this section.
```
